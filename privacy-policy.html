Grass - Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used, and disclosed by Wynd Network ("Company", "we", "us", "our") when you use our Grass application ("App"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

  1. Information We Collect

    2. We collect various types of information about you including:

    1. Personal Information: We may collect personal information such as your IP address and, potentially, your email address when you register for our services or otherwise communicate with us.
    2. Usage Information: We collect information about your usage of the App, including the amount of bandwidth shared.
  2. How We Use Information

    3. We use the information we collect to:

    1. Provide, maintain, and improve the App.
    2. Provide and deliver the services you request, process transactions, and send you related information.
    3. Send you technical notices, updates, and support messages.

  3. Sharing of Information

    4. We may share your information as follows:

    1. With vendors, consultants, and other service providers who need access to such information to carry out work on our behalf.

    3. In connection with, or during negotiations of, any merger, sale of company assets, financing or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company.
  4. Security

    5. We take reasonable measures to help protect personal information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

  5. Your Choices

    6. You can always choose not to disclose information. However, if you elect to do so, we may be unable to provide you with certain services.

  6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

    7. We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy.

  7. Contact Us

    8. If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at info@wynd.network.
